MSM receives special recognition from Red Cross

  • Posted on Thu, Aug 06 2015

    The Philippine Red Cross accorded the Silver Partner Award to Multinational Ship Management, Inc. in recognition of the company's support and contributions in preventing and alleviating human suffering during times of calamities.

     

    On the right corner is Red Cross chairman, Richard Gordon together with Alexis Ngan and Aileen Reyes of MSM.
